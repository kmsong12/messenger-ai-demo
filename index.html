How it works

Three simple steps to automate your group chat experience.

1. Photon iMessage Kit

Reads your Mac chat and sends recent messages to the agent in real-time.

2. Dedalus MCP Agent

Finds the real topic and picks the best model or tool for each request.

3. Messenger.ai

Returns links, info, images, or jokes directly to your group chat.
